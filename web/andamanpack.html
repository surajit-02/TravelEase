About Andaman

The **Andaman Islands** are a tropical paradise of turquoise waters, white sand beaches, and lush forests, located far from the Indian mainland in the Bay of Bengal. It's the perfect destination for relaxation, exploration, and underwater adventures.

From snorkeling at North Bay to scuba diving in Havelock Island, every activity in Andaman reveals vibrant coral reefs and marine life. You can relax at Radhanagar Beach — rated one of the best beaches in Asia — or explore history at the famous Cellular Jail.

Whether you're on a honeymoon, family vacation, or solo trip, the calm waters, untouched nature, and peaceful ambiance make Andaman a must-visit coastal escape.
