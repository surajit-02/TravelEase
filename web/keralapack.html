About Kerala

Kerala, often called “God’s Own Country,” is a tropical paradise nestled between the Arabian Sea and the Western Ghats. It’s known for its lush green landscapes, tranquil backwaters, pristine beaches, and rich cultural heritage.

A journey through Kerala offers something for everyone — relax on a houseboat in Alleppey, explore the misty tea gardens of Munnar, or get rejuvenated with a traditional Ayurvedic massage. Wildlife lovers can head to Periyar Wildlife Sanctuary, while beach enthusiasts will find peace in Kovalam and Varkala.

The cuisine, people, and traditions of Kerala make it one of India’s most inviting and soulful travel destinations. Whether you’re seeking relaxation, romance, or exploration, Kerala promises an unforgettable escape.
