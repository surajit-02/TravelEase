About Jaipur

Jaipur, famously known as the Pink City, is a vibrant blend of rich history, majestic architecture, and royal heritage. As the capital of Rajasthan, Jaipur is a gateway to India’s regal past — where palaces, forts, and bustling bazaars bring stories of kings and queens to life.

From the iconic Hawa Mahal with its honeycomb façade to the grand Amber Fort overlooking the hills, every corner of Jaipur is steeped in charm. Stroll through the City Palace, admire astronomical wonders at Jantar Mantar, or shop for colorful textiles, gemstones, and handcrafted souvenirs in the old city markets.

Jaipur offers more than just sightseeing — it’s an experience of royal culture, traditional Rajasthani cuisine, and timeless art. Whether you’re exploring ancient forts or enjoying a camel ride at sunset, Jaipur leaves every traveler enchanted.
