About Darjeeling

Darjeeling, perched high in the Eastern Himalayas, is a charming hill station known for its lush tea gardens, colonial charm, and sweeping views of the mighty Kanchenjunga — the third-highest mountain in the world.

Wake up to the cool mountain breeze, sip the world-famous Darjeeling tea straight from the source, and ride the iconic Darjeeling Himalayan Railway — a UNESCO World Heritage Site.

Popular attractions include the peaceful Peace Pagoda, bustling Mall Road, and the magical sunrise over the snow-capped peaks from Tiger Hill. Whether you're a nature lover, photographer, or simply seeking peace, Darjeeling offers an unforgettable retreat.
